Wishes

"I wish to see the ghost"
  • Pro: Ghost appears in front of the player as an event
  • Con: After the event, a hunt will start and player vision is darkened for the duration of the event and hunt
"I wish for activity"
  • Pro: Doubles all ghost activity for 2 min
  • Con: Breaker set to broken and front door locked for 2 min
"I wish for sanity"
  • Pro: Averages everyone's sanity to 50%
  • Con: Sets ghost's favorite room to a random room and increases current sanity drain by 50%
"I wish the ghost was trapped"
  • Pro: Ghost locked in its room for 1 min where it cannot hunt, roam, or do abilities
  • Con: Locks doors in the players room and hunt starts after the 1 min period (25% doors will unlock after grace period)
"I wish for life"
  • Pro: Revive a dead player
  • Con: 50% chance that the wishing player will die
"I wish to be safe"
  • Pro: Unblocks the nearest blocked hiding spot
  • Con: Breaks the light in that room and ghost is able to sense or hear the player from any distance
"I wish to leave"
  • Pro: Unlocks all exit doors (works during hunts)
  • Con: Player slowness and darkened vision for 5 secs
"I wish for knowledge"
  • Pro: Removes 1 incorrect evidence from the journal and crosses out associated ghosts
  • Con: Starts a hunt and player vision and audio is reduced the rest of that round
"I wish for [weather]"
  • Pro: Changes the weather to the one specified
  • Con: Drops sanity by 25%
"I wish for anything"
  • Randomly chooses one of the above wishes

Questions

"Where are you?"
  • Sanity Cost: 50%
  • Gives the current room of the ghost (not the favorite room)
Are you here? / Are you close?"
  • Sanity Cost: 20%
  • Tells if the ghost is in the same room as the player
"Where is the bone?"
  • Sanity Cost: 50%
  • Gives the room of the bone
"Hide and seek?"
  • Sanity Cost: 0% (breaks board)
  • Counts down from 5 and starts a hunt
"Do you respond to everyone?"
  • Sanity Cost: 20%
  • Replies with if the ghosts wants to be alone when using the spirit box
"How old are you?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns the current age of the ghost. If the number changes, the ghost is a Thaye
"When did you die?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns a random number
"What is my sanity?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • 'Healthy' (Sanity > 80%), 'Good' (60-80%), 'Average' (40-60%), 'Bad' (20-40%), 'Awful' (<20%)
"How insane am I?"
  • Sanity Cost: %
  • 'Not Very' (Sanity > 50%), 'Very' (25-50%), 'Insane' (<25%)
"Am I insane?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • 'No' (Sanity > 90%), 'Maybe' (20-90%), 'Yes' (<20%)
"How did you die?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns the ghost's cause of death
"How do you feel?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns a randomly selected emotion
"Why are you here?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns a randomly selected reason
"Knock Knock"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • "Whos there"
